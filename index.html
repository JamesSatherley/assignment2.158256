Pasifika Islands in 2080



"T" Art



The rich history behind the maori art of the Pasifika people is culturally significant. This art, show on the right, is located in the northern most point of the sunken islands. It is known simple as the "T" amongst the islanders. It is a must see for anyone traveling to the island, they say just being near it gives you great mana that any man would be envious of.



The sword of Makila



Makilas' sword is another aspect of the sunken Pasifika islands, located at the southeastern tip. It is said to be have been planted there by the saviour of the Pasifika 2022 Covid-19 Lambda variant. Absabuka was said to have push the sword into stone with his bare hands after developing the vaccine for the outbreak and being able to automatically give it to every Pasifika person in a number of seconds.
